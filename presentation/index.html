Why Would You Want To Go From Java To Clojure?

  1. Simpler, more elegant
    • Focus on the problem you are trying to solve not the ceremony around your implementation language
  2. Dynamic, but good performance
  3. Powerful extensibility
  4. FP and concurrency features

I won't cover most of this in this talk. So you have to take my word for it.
